Obvious is a 3yo chestnut Gelding from Australia trained by Greg Eurell, who is based at Cranbourne. It is sired by the stallion Toronado out of the dam No Place to Hide.

Obvious has managed to win 1 race in its career so far. On 17th Sep 2025 at Bendigo, Obvious was ridden by Ben Allen and scored its most significant win to date, getting the money in the $42500.00 3-Y-O Maiden Plte, defeating Grand Omaha.
